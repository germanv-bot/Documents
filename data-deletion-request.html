Data Deletion Request

Villa Advisory / Loyaltors Marketing Inc.

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data collected through our WhatsApp Business food ordering service. This page explains what data we hold, what gets deleted, and how to submit your request.

What Data We Hold

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your name and delivery address
  • Order history and cart contents
  • Message content and communication history
  • Preferred payment method declarations

How to Submit a Deletion Request

  1. Send an email to dev@villaadvisory.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your WhatsApp phone number (international format, e.g. +52 667 XXX XXXX)
  3. Provide proof of identity (e.g. screenshot of a message sent from your number)
  4. We confirm receipt within 5 business days and complete deletion within 30 days
Note: Some information may be retained where required by law (e.g. business records for a minimum of 7 years). We will inform you of any exceptions in our response.

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your profile, order history, and personal data are permanently removed from our systems
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
  • If you use the service again, a new profile will be created from scratch
