Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026  |  Effective Date: March 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Villa Advisory and/or Loyaltors Marketing Inc. ("Application," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information collected through our WhatsApp Business food ordering service, which allows customers to place orders at partner restaurants via WhatsApp. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Application.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Phone numbers (yours and recipient numbers for messaging)
  • Message content and communication history
  • Your name and delivery address information
  • Order history, cart contents, and preferred payment method declarations
  • Contact details and preferences
  • Account credentials and profile information

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • Interaction data with our Application
  • Message metadata (timestamps, delivery status, read receipts)
  • IP addresses and usage patterns
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

C. Information from Third Parties

  • WhatsApp Business Account information from Meta
  • Business verification details
  • Payment and billing information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Primary Purpose: Delivering WhatsApp Business food ordering services, including cart building, order confirmation, and delivery coordination
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns and optimizing functionality
  • Communication: Sending transactional messages, updates, and support communications
  • Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security violations
  • Analytics: Understanding user behavior and service performance
  • Business Operations: Managing accounts, payments, and customer support

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where applicable, we process personal data based on:

  • Contractual Necessity: Providing WhatsApp Business food ordering services
  • Legal Obligation: Complying with applicable laws and regulations
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving services, preventing fraud, and ensuring platform security
  • Consent: When you explicitly authorize specific data processing activities
  • Business Operations: Operating and maintaining our service

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

A. We Share Information With

  • Meta Platforms: As the WhatsApp service provider and data processor
  • Restaurant Partners: Order details are shared with the restaurant fulfilling your order. The restaurant acts as data controller for order-related information; Villa Advisory acts as data processor on their behalf
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting with operations, analytics, and customer support
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Partners: Only with explicit consent

B. We Do NOT

  • Sell personal data to third parties for marketing purposes
  • Share data without appropriate legal basis
  • Disclose sensitive information without authorization

6. Data Retention

  • Active Users: Duration of account use plus 30 days
  • Message Data: In compliance with WhatsApp's retention policies (typically 90 days for test messages)
  • Legal/Compliance: As required by applicable law (minimum 7 years for business records)
  • Inactive Accounts: Up to 12 months after account closure

Users may request deletion of their data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred, stored, and processed in the United States, Canada, and other countries where we or our service providers operate. These countries may have data protection laws different from your country of residence. Where applicable, we implement Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other approved mechanisms to ensure adequate protection for international transfers.

8. Your Privacy Rights

  • Right to access your personal data

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

B. CCPA Rights (California)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information collected

C. Mexico (LFPDPPP)

Under the Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión de los Particulares, users in Mexico have the right to exercise Derechos ARCO:

  • Acceso: Right to access your personal data
  • Rectificación: Right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Cancelación: Right to request deletion of your personal data
  • Oposición: Right to object to the processing of your personal data

D. Other Jurisdictions

Similar rights as provided by local data protection laws.

To exercise these rights, contact us at dev@villaadvisory.com.
